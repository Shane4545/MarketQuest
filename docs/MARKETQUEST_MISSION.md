# MarketQuest Mission

## What MarketQuest Is

MarketQuest is a **real-world-data AI market intelligence learning game**.

- Real market data, news, filings, currency movement, macro indicators, and outcomes
- Paper scoring only — no real-money trading
- AI agents and humans compete fairly using the same information
- Truth, learning, and competition over hype

**Mission:** Show young people they can use AI, business thinking, data, economics, and software to understand markets at a serious level — then compete fairly in a paper trading game using real-world information.

## What MarketQuest Is NOT

- Not a fake simulator with invented prices
- Not a broker
- Not real-money trading
- Not financial advice
- Not gambling
- Not a prediction-market app

## Product Feel

A mix of:

- Wall Street research terminal
- AI agent battle arena
- Business/economics classroom
- Live news radar
- Paper trading competition
- Strategy game
- Learning lab

## Honesty Rules

The app must **never** claim:

- Guaranteed profit
- Certain winners
- Real investment advice
- That students should use real money
- That AI always beats human experts
- That the system is better than a licensed advisor

The app **must prove things with game results**:

- Did this AI agent beat human players this week?
- Did it beat random picks?
- Did it beat SPY?
- Did it beat a simple momentum strategy?
- Did it beat its own past version?
- Which signal actually helped?
- Which signal was noise?

If the AI loses, show it. If data is stale, show it. If the prediction is weak, say so.

## Allowed Wording

- "MarketQuest AI beat the random baseline this week."
- "MarketQuest Ensemble beat SPY this week in paper scoring."
- "Momentum Agent lost to the Human Baseline this week."
- "News Agent performed poorly during stale-data periods."

## Forbidden Wording

- "Guaranteed to beat brokers."
- "Better than Edward Jones."
- "Best stocks ever."
- "Always wins."
- "Use this with real money."
